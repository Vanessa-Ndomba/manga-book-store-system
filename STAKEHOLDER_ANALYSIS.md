# Stakeholder Analysis for Manga Bookstore System

## Stakeholders

### 1. Customers  
- **Roles:** End users who purchase manga and related products.  
- **Concerns:** Availability of a diverse range of manga, pricing, ease of navigation on the site, delivery times, and customer service support.  
- **Pain Points:** Limited stock for popular titles, high shipping costs, and lack of information about new releases.  
- **Success Metrics:** Customer satisfaction ratings, repeat purchase rate, and average delivery time.

### 2. Store Managers  
- **Roles:** Oversee day-to-day operations.  
- **Concerns:** Inventory management, staff scheduling, sales performance, and customer satisfaction.  
- **Pain Points:** Difficulty tracking sales trends, managing staff, and handling customer complaints.  
- **Success Metrics:** Sales growth, employee satisfaction, and turnover rates.

### 3. Suppliers  
- **Roles:** Provide manga titles and related merchandise.  
- **Concerns:** Timely orders, payment reliability, and demand forecasting.  
- **Pain Points:** Unexpected changes in order quantities and late payments.  
- **Success Metrics:** Order fulfillment accuracy, payment timeliness, and relationship satisfaction scores.

### 4. Developers  
- **Roles:** Build and maintain the online platform.  
- **Concerns:** Website functionality, user experience, and system performance.  
- **Pain Points:** Technical issues affecting user experience, need for continuous updates, and integration with payment systems.  
- **Success Metrics:** System uptime, page load speed, and user engagement metrics.

### 5. Marketing Team  
- **Roles:** Promote the bookstore and its products.  
- **Concerns:** Effective communication strategies, customer reach, and marketing budget.  
- **Pain Points:** Competing with other bookstores, measuring campaign effectiveness, and ensuring brand visibility.  
- **Success Metrics:** Conversion rates, return on investment (ROI) for marketing campaigns, and social media engagement.

### 6. Investors  
- **Roles:** Provide funding and expect return on investment.  
- **Concerns:** Profitability and sustainability of the business.  
- **Pain Points:** Market competition and financial management concerns.  
- **Success Metrics:** Profit margins, overall growth, and market share.

## Conclusion  
By addressing the roles, concerns, pain points, and success metrics of these stakeholders, the manga bookstore system can strategically align its operations to enhance stakeholder satisfaction and drive growth.
