# Challenges in Balancing Stakeholder Needs

In managing the manga book store system project, various challenges were encountered while trying to balance the needs and expectations of different stakeholders. Below are some of the key challenges:

## 1. Divergent Interests
Different stakeholders had varying interests; for example, the marketing team wanted promotional features that could diverge from the development team's focus on stability and performance.

## 2. Communication Gaps
There were instances of miscommunication between stakeholders, leading to misunderstandings about project requirements and timelines.

## 3. Prioritization Conflicts
Choosing which stakeholder needs to prioritize often led to conflicts, especially when tight deadlines conflicted with comprehensive feature implementation.

## 4. Resource Limitations
Limited resources imposed constraints on fulfilling all stakeholder requests simultaneously, necessitating hard choices on what could be implemented and when.

## 5. Changing Requirements
Stakeholders occasionally changed their requirements mid-project, leading to scope creep and further complicating the balance of needs.

Reflecting on these challenges has provided valuable insights that can guide better stakeholder management in future projects.
